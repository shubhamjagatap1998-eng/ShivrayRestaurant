Our Heritage

A tribute to the Maratha legacy

छत्रपती शिवाजी महाराजांच्या वारसाचा सन्मान

Swarajya on Your Plate तुमच्या ताटावर स्वराज्य

Named after the great Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Shivray Restaurant brings you the authentic flavors of Maharashtra. Our recipes are inspired by the royal kitchens of the Maratha empire, passed down through generations.

From the fiery Kolhapuri spices to the delicate Malvani coastal flavors, from the hearty Varhadi cuisine to the royal Deccan platters - every dish is a celebration of our rich heritage.

We use hand-pounded spices, pure ghee, and traditional cooking methods to recreate the authentic taste that our ancestors savored.
